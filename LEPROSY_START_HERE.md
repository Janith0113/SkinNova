## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Will this guarantee medical accuracy?**
A: Yes - all data comes from WHO, CDC, and ILA. We cite every source. Always include "Consult your healthcare provider" disclaimers.

**Q: How often do I need to update the knowledge base?**
A: Check WHO and CDC quarterly for updates. Major treatment protocols rarely change, but it's good practice.

**Q: Can I add my own data?**
A: Only if it comes from WHO, CDC, or peer-reviewed journals with Tier 1 organization verification.

**Q: Do I need to modify the JSON files?**
A: You can add comments and localize content (language, regional references), but stick to the verified structure.

**Q: What about patient privacy?**
A: Knowledge base contains NO patient data. All medical facts are general education only.
